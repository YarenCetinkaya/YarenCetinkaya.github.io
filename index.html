BIOGRAPHY

I was born on 24 September 2000 in Silifke district of Mersin Province, mediterrean Turkey. I am the only
child of my family. My parents are both teachers.

At fifth grade, I went to America and lived there for one year. I learned to speak English while I went
to their school there. In middle my family moved to Konya Province. Then, I went to Meram Science High
School for 4 years.

All these years, even if I never get educated in art, I self-taught myself in traditional art then in
University I began to do digital art then sell my arts as a freelancer in https://www.bionluk.com.
Because I always like to explore computers, I choose Computer Engineering as a career choice.

Unlike my thoughts were like this, I went to Electrical Electronics Engineering for one year
in AYBU(Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University). Then I transfered to Computer Engineering again, in AYBU.
After I focused on Computer Engineering, I decided to specialize in Game Industry like I said before.
I began to take courses on Game engines and also I took my digital art to an advanced level. I began
to join Game Jams. Game Jams are some events that you have a theme and 48 to 72 hours to make a game
in this limited time. So far, I participated in two different Game Jam teams and also we produced two
different games. itch.io is the social site for uploading small games like Game Jam games.
